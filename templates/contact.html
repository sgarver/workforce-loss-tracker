Media & Press

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to data accuracy or website functionality, please indicate "URGENT" in the subject line.

Before Contacting

Please check our FAQ page for answers to common questions. You can also search our existing layoff data to see if the information you're looking for is already available.

Note: Workforce Loss Tracker is a data aggregation service. We do not have direct relationships with companies listed in our database and cannot provide employment advice or job placement services.
